Return on investment
What the digital investment unlocks — annually
Three independent revenue streams the improved site enables. Figures use conservative, moderate, and aggressive assumptions on donation uplift, trip fill rate, and corporate bookings. All financial figures are Sample estimates — Walker to validate with real donation history.
Stream 1
📣 Donation uplift
A redesigned site with clear CTAs, impact data, and trust signals typically increases online giving 15–35%. CAMINO's estimated annual donation base: ~$80,000–$120,000.
Conservative: +$10,000/yr
Moderate: +$22,000/yr
Aggressive: +$40,000/yr
Stream 2
✈️ Trip registrations
Each additional trip participant = $1,100 in fees ($600 R&B + $500 project fund). If the trip portals and marketing drive 10–20 extra participants/year above current enrollment.

Moderate: +$13,200/yr (12 people)
Aggressive: +$22,000/yr (20 people)
