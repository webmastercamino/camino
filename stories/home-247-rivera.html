Home #247 · 20 de Julio, Honduras

The Rivera Family

Oklahoma Mission Team · 2026 Sponsored by Stobtown Freewill Baptist Church, Lone Grove, OK
Before
After

CAMINO Home #247 was handed over to Julian and Ana Rivera and their daughter Genesis in the community of 20 de Julio — built by our Oklahoma Mission Team in partnership with Stobtown Freewill Baptist Church of Lone Grove, Oklahoma.

The Rivera family is woven into the heart of 20 de Julio. Julian's father, Don Juan, is a community leader who has given his time and labor to several CAMINO projects — including work in the nearby community of Barrio Reyes. Julian's sister, Reina, received a CAMINO home the previous year with this same team. This year she came back — not as a recipient, but as a builder — painting the base colors for the community's new Welcome Wall alongside the Oklahoma volunteers.

That's the CAMINO pattern at its best: families who received help becoming part of the team that helps others.

"The family has been very excited by the work that the team has done for their family and the community."
