Your gift in action

What your donation builds

Here's how different gift amounts translate directly into work on the ground in Honduras.

$50

Construction materials

Covers cement, rebar, or roofing sheets for part of a home — the building blocks of a family's shelter.

$500

A week of local labor

Pays a full week of wages for local Honduran builders — creating jobs while building homes.

$2,000

A complete family home

Builds a permanent, safe, fully completed home from foundation to roof — changing a family's life forever.
