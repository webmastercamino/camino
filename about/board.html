Our Leadership

Grant Funding & Transparency

CAMINO actively pursues grant funding to support our programs in Honduras. We are committed to full transparency with our funders and the public.

Is your foundation interested in supporting CAMINO's work in Honduras?

CAMINO welcomes grant funding for our core programs and emerging initiatives:

  • Mission trips — volunteer coordination, travel, and site logistics for groups of 4–25
  • Community development — home construction ($5,500/home), water access, and infrastructure
  • Future: vocational training — skills development pilot launching 2026–2027

Get involved

Join the Board

Interested in serving on the CAMINO board? We welcome applicants who share our passion for Honduras and nonprofit governance. Board members serve two-year terms and meet quarterly — with optional on-site visits to Honduras.

We particularly welcome candidates with backgrounds in law, accounting, fundraising, communications, healthcare, or international development — and those with a personal connection to Honduras or faith-based mission work.
