Our Leadership

Get involved

Join the Board

Interested in serving on the CAMINO board? We welcome applicants who share our passion for Honduras and nonprofit governance. Board members serve two-year terms and meet quarterly — with optional on-site visits to Honduras.

We particularly welcome candidates with backgrounds in law, accounting, fundraising, communications, healthcare, or international development — and those with a personal connection to Honduras or faith-based mission work.
