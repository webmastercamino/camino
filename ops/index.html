💊 Health Reminders for Honduras

Dengue fever — Endemic. Wear DEET repellent at dusk and dawn. Long sleeves when possible.
Water safety — Drink bottled water only. Avoid ice unless confirmed purified. No raw produce washed in tap water.
Sun & heat — Cortés / Tegucigalpa can exceed 35°C. Drink 3L water daily on build days. Rest in shade midday.
Recommended vaccines — Hep A, Typhoid, Tdap, Influenza. Rabies for animal-contact risk. Malaria prophylaxis if traveling to Mosquitia.
Altitude (Tegucigalpa) — ~1000 m. Most people adapt in 24h. If dizzy or headache, rest and hydrate before physical work.
Travel insurance — Confirm each participant has coverage including medical evacuation. Keep policy numbers on hand.
