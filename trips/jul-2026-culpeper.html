Weather

Northern Honduras in July averages 85–95°F (29–35°C) with high humidity. July is rainy season — expect afternoon and evening showers most days. Wear lightweight, moisture-wicking fabrics and bring a light rain jacket or poncho.

Money

The local currency is the Honduran lempira (HNL). ATMs exist in San Pedro Sula. USD is accepted at most hotels and larger stores. Bring small bills for markets and tips.

Health & vaccinations

Consult your doctor 4–6 weeks before travel. Recommended: Hepatitis A, Typhoid. Drink only bottled or purified water. In July (rainy season), use DEET insect repellent consistently — mosquito activity is higher.

Communication

Most U.S. carriers offer affordable international plans. Local SIM cards are available at SAP airport. Team house has WiFi. CAMINO staff are reachable 24/7 via WhatsApp during your trip.

What to leave home

Leave expensive jewelry, flashy electronics, and large amounts of cash. Don't bring more than you can carry. CAMINO will store valuables at the team house.

Cultural respect

Dress modestly, especially at church. Learn a few Spanish phrases — locals deeply appreciate the effort. Ask before photographing individuals or families.

Emergency protocols

1
Medical issue: Notify CAMINO field staff immediately. For minor injuries, the team house has a first-aid kit. For anything serious, CAMINO staff will transport you to Hospital Leonardo Martínez in San Pedro Sula.
2
Lost passport: Contact CAMINO staff, then call the U.S. Embassy in Tegucigalpa. Your flight booking confirmation and a photo ID copy (carry one separately) will help expedite an emergency travel document.
3
Separation from group: Stay in a public, well-lit area. Call CAMINO staff immediately. Do not wander or attempt to navigate independently — wait for pickup.
4
Security concern: Trust your instincts and alert CAMINO staff. Our field team has deep local knowledge and will adjust plans immediately. Do not engage with any perceived threat.
5
Flight cancellation / travel disruption: Contact CAMINO staff before rebooking anything. We have a protocol for extended stays and can assist with accommodations and rebooking.
