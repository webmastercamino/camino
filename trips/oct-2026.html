Preparation

Know before you go

October is transitioning out of rainy season — expect warm days and occasional showers early in the week.

Packing checklist

Passport (valid 6+ months past return date)
Travel insurance documents
Work clothes (4–5 sets)
Light rain jacket or poncho
Closed-toe work boots
Work gloves
Sunscreen (SPF 50+)
Insect repellent (DEET-based)
Prescription medications (extra supply)
Reusable water bottle
$50–100 USD cash
Phone charger (Type A plugs)

Weather in October

Late rainy season transitioning to dry. Expect 80–90°F (27–32°C). Some morning showers are possible early in the week, but weather is generally excellent by mid-week.

Fall is a great season

October brings lush green landscapes and pleasant evenings. It's one of the most scenic times to visit Northern Honduras. Less crowded at tourist sites too.

Health

Hepatitis A and Typhoid recommended. October can have higher mosquito activity — bring strong repellent. Drink only bottled or purified water.

Communication

Team house has WiFi. CAMINO staff reachable 24/7 via WhatsApp. Most U.S. carriers have affordable Honduras plans.
